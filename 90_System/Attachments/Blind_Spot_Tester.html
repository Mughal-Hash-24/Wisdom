Physiological Blind Spot Tester

How to use:

  1. Sit about 20 inches (50 cm) away from the screen.
  2. Close your LEFT eye.
  3. Focus your RIGHT eye strictly on the Cross (+) on the left.
  4. Slowly move your head closer to the screen while keeping focus on the Cross.
  5. At a specific distance, the Dot (•) on the right will disappear.

(To test the Left eye: Close Right eye, focus on Dot, Cross will disappear.)
